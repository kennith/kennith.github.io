Google 中文 Web Fonts

I always thought Google Web Fonts helped making the WWW a much beter looking without requiring developers embedding their customize fonts in their site. I think the Asian font should be next and I was wondering how will Google solve this problem.

I found there is an early access to other fonts in Google Web Fonts. I found there are Chinese fonts available to use. I am very excited about it.

The followings are the five currently available. They look pretty, but each of them are over 4mb. It is certainly not ready to use for mobile, but at least we can preview them if your device is connected to the high speed Wi-Fi.

Please be patient when you load this page. It might takes more than 10 seconds before the correct Chinese fonts to be displayed

Enjoy!
